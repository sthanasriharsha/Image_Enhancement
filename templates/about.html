About This Project

This web app demonstrates classic and modern digital image processing techniques, with a focus on low-light image enhancement.

Built with Python, Flask, OpenCV, and NumPy. Hosted on Render.

✨ CLAHE

Contrast Limited Adaptive Histogram Equalisation — best for local contrast improvement.

🌙 Retinex

Illumination normalisation based on the Retinex theory of human vision.

γ Gamma Correction

Simple power-law transform to brighten dark images with adjustable strength.

🔇 Denoising

Fast Non-Local Means denoising to clean up noise introduced by low-light capture.

🔍 Filters

Edge detection, Gaussian blur, and unsharp sharpening via convolution kernels.

📊 Histogram

Per-channel RGB histogram visualisation and equalisation.
